Delicious fondue

This Cheese Fondue with grilled bread is such a fun, quick, and easy appetizer to make on the grill this Summer!

This cheese fondue recipe is always so good! I've been making it exactly like this since the early '70s. I like to serve it with cubes of French bread for dipping, but baby potatoes, bell peppers, and sliced apples also work great!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ pound shredded Swiss cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Gruyere cheese
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, cut into 1 inch cubes

Steps

  1. Bring wine to a simmer in a fondue pot.
  2. Stir in flour and nutmeg until well combined.
  3. Add Swiss and Gruyere cheeses, 1/4 pound at a time, mixing after each addition until cheese is melted. Season with salt.
  4. Serve with cut-up French bread.
  5. Enjoy!
